Trying to get away? - Oliver Corino

Two stories about transcendence. 1. Sleazy Boarding - a short story about a group of dressed up people trying to go on a city break to Prague. 2. Savage Gardens (19:00 mins) - The story of how Canary Wharf ended up in the derelict docklands of East London. Extract from the first chapter of Architectural Fathers by Canary Wharf.

Oliver Corino is a writer based in London. Currently he is completing his first novel Architectural Fathers by Canary Wharf.

Recorded by M. J. Harding
