Threatened Music

Ralph

Ralph works in social care. He plays in the bands Permission and Subdued. He also plays improvised music in private.

Moving between jazz, hardcore punk, poetry and electronics; traversing continents and timelines from the mid-twenith century to the current moment, Ralph's show makes unexpected connections between a seemingly eclectic variety of practitioners from Archie Sheep to Orden Mundial.

With thanks to James Janco for production.
