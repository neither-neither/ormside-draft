Landlocked: music from and related to the films of Black Audio Film Collective - Jim Janco

Featuring music by Trevor Mathison, Hallucinator, Jah Shaka, Creation Rebel, Rufige Kru, DJ Spooky, Mark Stewart and Maffia, Test Dept and Coil.

Jim Janco is a musician based in London. He runs the People Drift nights at Ormside and Venue MOT.
