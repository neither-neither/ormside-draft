Bradford Bailey - The Hum

Bradford Bailey (b.1978, USA) is a writer currently based in London. His work - published extensively in the form of articles, essays, and liner notes - is dedicated to ideas surrounding sound and music, offering particular focus to the exploration of pathways toward mutual aid, shared and cross-cultural understanding, collectivism, and community. He is also the writer of The Hum, a blog dedicated to supporting historic and contemporary music which exists beyond the realms of mainstream interest.
