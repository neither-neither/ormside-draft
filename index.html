Uprising: South London Industrial Map

21st March 2021 4pm

Map & audio guide exploring the history of Industrial music in South London during the 1980's, uncovering the network of venues and squats that formed the foundation of the scene in the area, and the music that beat a defiant soundtrack against an era of cultural oppression, economic decline and aggressive corporate political organisation.

A research project by Paul Purgas.
